# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and technically specific.
- Critique ideas, workflows, and code without attacking people.
- Assume good intent, but ask for clarification when ambiguity affects quality or safety.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce issues and review changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith engagement
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission
- Knowingly submitting malicious, deceptive, or unsafe skill content

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these standards. Repeated or serious violations may result in removal from the project.
